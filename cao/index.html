COURSES

Step-by-step courses to bring you the best tips directly from the pros, so you can master the techniques, reproduce the recipes and start creating your own. Wide range of topics and skill levels, from beginner to advanced. Download recipes, ask questions and share your creations with your peers today. 1 new course released every week!

01

Confectionery

Chocolate is an ingredient like no other. Discover how to work with it and reveal all its potential through these confectionary courses designed to unveil step by step the chocolatier's best kept secrets.

02

Plated dessert

The chefs bring you their best recipes and ideas for you to create enticing plated desserts. From simple to more complex creations, always aiming to be a feast for the tastebuds and the eyes.

03

Bakery

As artisanal bakery grows, so does the use of quality chocolate in baked goods. Learn how to incorporate great chocolate into your baking with the guidance of professional bakers eager to share their knowledge with you.

04

What's new

A new course is added every week. This is the place to never miss out on what has been posted recently so you can continue learning and exploring the world of chocolate!

05

Fundamentals

Learn the fundamentals of working with chocolate in a detailed interactive video format and master the techniques that will contribute to achieving exceptional results.

06

Pastry

From classic French recipes and techniques, to trendy pastry creations from around the world, learn and expand your pastry skills with the guidance of expert chefs.

TECHNIQUES

Techniques are the foundation of any recipe. Learn, practice and master the techniques from the simplest to the most advanced recipes so you can have the freedom to create your own.

01

Tempering chocolate

To create the perfect chocolate products you simply need to know how to temper or pre-crystallise your chocolate. Learn how to temper using different methods to suit your needs and achieve perfect results every time.

02

Enrobed chocolate

Know how to properly choose your chocolate and how to unleash your creativity when enrobing chocolates with our tutorials.

03

Molded chocolate

Watch our chefs and get the basics of moulding chocolate figures.

04

Chocolate mousse

Choose your favorite way to make a chocolate mousse using a homemade cream or with ready-to-use products.

05

Chocolate glaze, sauce & spray

Find out how to give the perfect final touch to all your moulded bonbons and pastries.

06

Chocolate tips and tricks

Ever had questions about chocolate fluidity, storage, fat boom and sugar bloom? See what are the advices of our chefs.

07

Hollow Figures

Hollow figures can make your window pop or boost sales during special celebrations. Learn more about how to achieve the perfect moulding and beautiful decoration of hollow figures.
