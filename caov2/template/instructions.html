Common Questions & Tutorials

Still have a struggle with the template?
Fell free to email us

How to change the main color palette?

  • Open the the Style Guide page
  • Choose the color you want to change
  • Edit the global swatch in the background settings
Watch Video

How to set a different page as your homepage?

  • Open the Pages panel (press "P" on the keyboard)
  • Open the settings of the page you want to make your new homepage
  • Set the Parent Folder to "None" and click to Save button
  • Then open the page settings again and click "Make 'This Page' Your Homepage"
Watch Video

How to change Nav Bar background on scroll?

  • Open the Interactions panel (press "H" on the keyboard)
  • Add new Page Trigger called "While page is scrolling"
  • Select "Change Nav Bar Background" animation from the list
